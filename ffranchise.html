ABOUT COMPANY

Largest Restaurants Chain of the country

First Fiddle Restaurants, one of the world’s largest F&B company in the casual dining sector, is headed by Priyank Sukhija. From Lazeez Affair, the first brand conceived in 1999 to Dragonfly Experience and Plum By Bent Chair, its latest ventures, it has brought a wave of innovation in the hospitality industry. It has been lauded with an endless number of awards by ‘Times Nightlife’, ‘GQ’, ‘Indian Restaurant Awards’- to name a few. With more than 35 brands under its name such as Lord Of The Drinks, Tamasha, The Flying Saucer Café, JLWA, it has grown manifold in the past 20 years and continues to do so.

Why to choose a restaurant franchise?

THE GAME CHANGER OF THE INDUSTRY

A prolific restaurateur, Priyank Sukhija has been successful in bringing a wave of innovation in the restaurant industry. Born in a family of lawyers, he became the only businessman in the family, he opened the fine-dining restaurant Lazeez Affaire at Malcha Marg in 1999.

Beginning his journey with Lazeez Affaire, he has now been able to create the most talked about brands in the hospitality industry- Lord of the Drinks, Plum by Bent Chair, Dragonfly Experience, JLWA, Teddy Boy, Tamasha, The Flying Saucer Cafe and many more.Coming from an era where people either opted for fine dining or clubbing, Priyank has been a true pioneer in building and popularizing the casual dining. Café Out of the Box was not only the first casual dining space in Hauz Khas Village of its size and concept but also a turning point for Hauz Khas Village to become what it stands in all its glory today- a great hub for restaurants, cafes, and bars.

For the upcoming months, Priyank has both national and international projects in the pipelines. Bringing different brands like The Flying Saucer Café to Mumbai, Priyank is also working on creating newness with the hospitality space with upcoming brands like Miso Sexy in Bangalore and Diablo in Delhi.
With 20 glorious years already shining in Priyank Sukhija’s career map, he is determined to have many more years shining brighter than the last. Not only is he at the top of his game, but also, he is also constantly pushing the boundaries of innovation to stay there!

01

Franchise own and Franchise Operated (FOFO)

The franchise owner will have the complete ownership and responsibility of maintaining all operational activities from set up of interiors to daily operations. First Fiddle Restaurants will provide full support on how to manage the operations and also have regular audits to check if the company processes are followed.

02

Franchise own company-operated (FOCO)

The franchise owner and First Fiddle Restaurant together work together where the brand guidelines are maintained. The company will manage the marketing and provide with all the help that is required starting from set up of interiors to daily operations.

03

Direct Investment

In Direct Investment, you acquire a lasting interest in terms of shares of the brand you choose to be associated with First Fiddle Restaurants.
